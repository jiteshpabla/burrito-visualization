Detailed burrito reviews

Jitesh Pabla - jpabla1@asu.edu - 1217310679

This visualization is based on average reviews of the burritos from the restaurants on the X axis. The reviews are for the burritos in general, only for the tortilla, only for the meat etc. The different review values are represented by minimal images representing the ingredient/property of the burrito (like tortilla or uniformity etc.). The marks used are: line and point. The channels used are: position, shape and color, and opacity.
